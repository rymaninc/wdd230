Target Audience

The target audience are residents of Cainta, Rizal, businesses enterprises engaged in manufacturing, services, retailers, education, and employment. Additional audiences are busines owners of Smal-Medium-Enterprises and thoise who are willing to contribute in the development of businesses of the local town.
