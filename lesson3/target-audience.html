Target Audience

The target audience is foreign and local tourists, residents, government units, and business enterprises engaged in tourism, hotels, and restaurants. Other audiences are business owners of Smal-Medium-Enterprises and those who are willing to contribute to the economic development of businesses in the province.

Persona Group:

Entrepreneurs or Business Owners

Name: Samuel T. Angara

Occupation: President of a group of companies of hotels and resorts in the Philippines, Singapore, Malaysia, and Indonesia.

Demographics: Age is 58 years old, post graduate degree holder, former politician, and family is known in the business industry for decades.

Goals and task to complete from the site: Establish network among with the business owners, entrepreneurs, and potential business partners.

Environment: Physical environment is demanding that requires a lot of time management. Social environment is mostly extrovert due to frequent business meetings, travel, and networking. Uses high-end devices and fast internet connections.


Tourist

Scenarios

Samuel T. Angara plans to expand his hotel and restaurant businesses in coastal areas. Because of his busy schedule, he is looking for content online regarding tourist destinations in the coastal regions of the Philippines. He is scouting potential locations to invest some of his money.


Martina Santos is an operations manager of an IT company. Her hobby includes vlogging, traveling, and sharing stories through social media. Her passion is to travel and tell stories. She currently has two million followers on social media. Her followers are waiting for the new destination she will share each weekend. But she also loves nature and wants to help the communities in coastal areas. She promotes these places by visiting and describing to her followers what the site looks like, the rates of the hotels, what restaurants to eat at, and where they should stay.
